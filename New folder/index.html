A Carousel

A carousel or carrousel (mainly North American English),merry-go-round (international) or roundabout (British English) is a type of amusement ride consisting of a rotating circular platform with seats for riders.

The Seats

The "seats" are traditionally in the form of rows of wooden horses or other animals mounted on posts, many of which are moved up and down by gears to simulate galloping, to the accompaniment of looped circus music.

Creatures

Carousels are commonly with horses but may include a variety of mounts like pigs, zebras, tigers, or mythological creatures such as dragons or unicorns. Sometimes, chair-like or bench-like seats it can be shaped like aeroplanes or cars.
